# Altecho Driving School - Luxury Premium Redesign

## Project Overview

Transform the Altecho Driving School website from cyberpunk/neon aesthetic to a luxury premium automotive design that conveys sophistication, trust, and premium service.

## Design Direction

### Color Palette
| Role | Color | Usage |
|------|-------|-------|
| Primary | `#d4af37` (Gold) | CTAs, accents, highlights |
| Primary Light | `#f5c542` | Hover states, secondary accents |
| Background Dark | `#0a0a0a` | Primary backgrounds |
| Background Secondary | `#141414` | Cards, sections |
| Background Accent | `#1a1a1a` | Elevated surfaces |
| Text Primary | `#ffffff` | Headings |
| Text Secondary | `#a0a0a0` | Body text |
| Text Muted | `#666666` | Captions |
| Navy Accent | `#1e3a5f` | Trust sections |

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" (elegant serif for headings)
- **Body Font**: "DM Sans" (refined sans-serif for body)
- **Accent Font**: "Cormorant Garamond" (for quotes, testimonials)

### Spacing & Layout
- Generous whitespace (80px+ section padding)
- Max content width: 1400px
- Grid: 12-column system
- Border radius: 8px (subtle), 16px (cards)

## Components Specification

### 1. Navigation
- Fixed transparent header, becomes solid dark on scroll
- Logo in gold accent
- Links in refined sans-serif
- "Book Now" CTA in gold with hover animation

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-screen immersive design
- High-quality car/driver imagery (parallax)
- Elegant typography with subtle gold gradient
- Two CTAs: Primary (gold), Secondary (outlined)
- Scroll indicator with smooth animation

### 3. Services Section
- 3-4 column grid on desktop
- Cards with subtle elevation, gold border accents
- Premium iconography
- Hover: lift effect with gold glow

### 4. Testimonials Section
- Large quote styling
- Customer photos with elegant frames
- Star ratings in gold
- Horizontal scroll carousel

### 5. Pricing Section
- 3-tier layout (Basic, Premium, Elite)
- Premium tier highlighted with gold border
- Feature lists with checkmarks
- CTA buttons

### 6. Areas Served
- Map or list format
- Clean card design

### 7. CTA Section
- Dramatic background
- Compelling copy
- Gold CTA button

### 8. Footer
- Multi-column layout
- Gold accents on headings
- Social links with elegant hover
- Newsletter signup

## 3D Elements Specification

### Hero 3D
- Replace neon shapes with metallic car elements
- Floating premium car silhouettes
- Warm gold/white particle effects
- Smooth auto-rotation

### Services 3D Background
- Geometric shapes with metallic materials
- Gold and white color scheme
- Subtle distortion effects

### Global 3D
- Use R3F with warm lighting
- Environment: "studio" or "city" with warm tones

---

## Animation Specification

- Page transitions: 0.6s ease-out
- Scroll reveals: 0.4s with 0.1s stagger
- Hover states: 0.3s ease
- Scroll-triggered fade-up animations
- Parallax on hero images (subtle)

---

## Responsive Breakpoints

- Desktop: 1200px+
- Tablet: 768px - 1199px
- Mobile: < 768px

---

## Success Criteria

1. Premium, luxury feel that matches high-end automotive brands
2. Clear visual hierarchy with gold as accent
3. 3D elements enhance without overwhelming
4. Mobile-responsive with touch-friendly interactions
5. Fast load times with optimized images
6. Accessibility compliant (contrast, keyboard nav)
